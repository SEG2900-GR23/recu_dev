# Style Guide for Roomie

## Overall Look and Feel of Roomie
The overall look and feel should be minimalist and flat. We will avoid 3D buttons and 3D metal looks. The website should be calm and gentle for the user.

## Colours
For Roomie, colours expose our personality to the customers and represent that we are calm and pleasing. These are our color swatches:

- #583E7F
- #DEC9FF
- #B07CFF
- #6F647F
- #8D63CC

## Iconography
We will focus on using icons that have a 'flat' design, which adheres to the overall look and feel. When selecting an icon, please select the correct icon for the job and keep the following in mind:

## Typography
We will be using Roboto from Google Fonts.

## Links
Links will not be underlined and should change color when a user hovers over it.

## Buttons
Buttons will be flat designed using the chosen colors outlined above.
